# Revision Policy — Kanto Web Design

All packages include **2 rounds of revisions**. Each round covers:

- **Content changes** — text, headlines, descriptions, pricing updates
- **Color adjustments** — accent colors, background tones, section styling
- **Image swaps** — replacing placeholder images with client-provided photos

## What counts as 1 round

Any combination of the above submitted together = 1 round. Send all your changes at once and we'll apply them in one pass.

## Out of scope (change orders)

These are NOT covered by the 2 revision rounds and require a separate quote:

- Adding new pages beyond the original scope
- Adding new sections to existing pages (e.g., adding a "Team" or "FAQ" section mid-project)
- Adding features not in the original agreement (booking system, online store, membership area)
- Structural redesign after mockup approval
- Changing the template after work has started (Generic tier)

## The process

1. You approve the mockup
2. You pay 50% deposit
3. We build your site
4. You review → 1st round of changes
5. You review again → 2nd round of changes
6. You pay the remaining 50%
7. Your site goes live

Reasonable, good-faith adjustments that take under 30 minutes are on us. We're not here to nickel-and-dime you — this policy exists to prevent scope creep on large builds, not to charge for small fixes.
